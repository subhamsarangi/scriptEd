Privacy Policy

Akshara — Learn Odia Script

Overview

Akshara is a free, offline app for learning the Odia script. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect — which is essentially none.

Data We Do Not Collect

Akshara does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-ins, and no forms asking for your name, email, or any other personal details.

The app works entirely offline after installation. No data is ever sent to any server.

Local Storage

Your quiz scores and flashcard session progress are stored locally on your device only, using your browser's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Akshara does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs. There are no ads in the app.

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type) as part of their infrastructure. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Children's Privacy

Akshara is suitable for all ages, including children. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at github.com/subhamsarangi/scriptEd.
